Daniel Island, SC

Location

Daniel Island is a coastal town that can be found a little over 14 miles from Charleston, SC.

About

Weather

The weather found in Daniel Island can be some of the best on the East Coast. However, be sure to look at the weather report, as it can get a little unpredictable at times.

Population

Daniel Island's population as reported in 2021 is around 10,000, with many new residents as time goes on.

Sports

The most popular sports team in Daniel Island are the Battling Bishops, the football team belonging to Bishop England High School.

Things To Do

  • Beautiful parks and nature walks
  • Numerous community events
  • Country Club that includes:
    • Pool
    • Gym
    • Golfing
    • Dining
    • And more!
