The Living Tombstone Fan Webpage

The Living Tombstone is an electronic artist that began their YouTube channel on May 21, 2011. TLS has made a variety of songs, beginning with fan songs for things like Five Nights at Freddy's and My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic, and later branching out to create more original works.
